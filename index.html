AI in Practice

Where I Apply AI
to Real Marketing Problems

I have built and used AI tools where they genuinely save time and improve decisions, not as a label, but as a working part of my process.

01
Campaign Insight Chatbot
Built using OpenAI API and Google Vertex AI to pull real-time campaign performance data and surface insights on demand. Reduced the time spent on manual reporting and made it faster to spot anomalies and act on them.
02
Automated Reporting Workflows
Set up automated pipelines that collect, structure, and summarise campaign data across clients. This freed up hours each week that were previously spent on repetitive reporting tasks and allowed more focus on optimisation.
03
Prompt Frameworks for Analysis
Designed structured prompt systems for diagnosing campaign performance, identifying patterns across CPA, CPL, CPS, and CPV models, and generating actionable recommendations quickly during client review calls.
04
Predictive Budget Modelling
Currently building a predictive model using Python and AI to forecast campaign outcomes and guide budget decisions across accounts. Early results are informing how we set margin targets and allocate spend.
In Progress

CRM Enrichment
Pulling campaign source data and publisher attribution into CRM records automatically for smarter segmentation.
Churn Signals
Identifying early churn risk in subscription and fintech accounts to trigger re-engagement before it is too late.
Attribution Modelling
Multi-touch attribution across pricing models to accurately connect campaign spend to actual revenue generated.
Conversational Qualification
Chatbot flows that qualify leads and hand off to sales with full context, cutting response time significantly.
